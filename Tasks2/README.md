# Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This dashboard was created using the Superstore dataset to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, regional trends, and time-based patterns. It also includes a short-term sales forecast to support decision-making.

---

## Steps Performed

**1. Data Understanding**

* Reviewed the dataset structure
* Identified key variables such as Sales, Profit, Quantity, Segment, Category, Region, Ship Mode, and Order Date

---

**2. Data Preparation**

* Checked for missing or inconsistent values
* Validated data types (numerical, categorical, date fields)
* Prepared data for analysis

---

**3. KPI Creation**

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Delivery Time

---

**4. Data Visualization**
Developed visuals to analyze:

* Sales by Segment
* Sales by Category
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Sales by Region
* Sales and Profit by State
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Monthly Profit Trend
* Sales by Ship Mode

---

**5. Trend Analysis**

* Evaluated monthly sales and profit performance
* Identified seasonal patterns, including Q4 sales spike

---

**6. Forecasting**

* Built a 15-day sales forecast
* Analyzed expected short-term demand behavior

---

**7. Insight Generation**

* Identified major revenue drivers
* Highlighted regional performance differences
* Assessed operational preferences

---

## Outcome

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance, enabling better understanding of trends, opportunities, and areas for improvement.
